Recommended Itineraries

Classic China Journey

14 Days | Beijing - Xi'an - Chengdu - Shanghai

Days 1-4: Beijing

Explore the Forbidden City, Great Wall, Temple of Heaven, and experience local hutong life.

Days 5-7: Xi'an

Visit the Terracotta Warriors, Ancient City Wall, and indulge in delicious street food.

Days 8-10: Chengdu

Meet the giant pandas, taste authentic Sichuan cuisine, and visit Leshan Giant Buddha.

Days 11-14: Shanghai

Experience the Bund, modern Pudong, and take day trips to nearby water towns.

Natural Wonders Tour

10 Days | Zhangjiajie - Guilin - Yunnan

Days 1-3: Zhangjiajie

Hike through the surreal stone pillars of Zhangjiajie National Forest Park and walk on the glass bridge.

Days 4-6: Guilin & Yangshuo

Cruise down the Li River, cycle through karst landscapes, and explore the beautiful caves.

Days 7-10: Yunnan

Discover the stunning landscapes of Lijiang, the Tiger Leaping Gorge, and the vibrant culture of Dali.
