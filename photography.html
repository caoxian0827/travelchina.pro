Photography Tips

Capturing Misty Mountains

The karst mountains and mist-filled valleys of places like Zhangjiajie and Guilin are iconic China landscapes. To capture them at their best:

  • Shoot during early morning (5-8 AM) when mist is most prevalent
  • Use a polarizing filter to cut through haze and enhance colors
  • Consider a telephoto lens to compress layers of mountains
  • Include scale elements like boats or trees to show the grandeur

Urban Photography in China

China's cities offer a fascinating blend of ancient traditions and futuristic architecture. Here's how to capture this contrast:

  • Shoot during the "blue hour" just after sunset for balanced lighting
  • Look for juxtaposition of old and new elements
  • Use wide-angle lenses to capture towering skyscrapers
  • Explore alleyways and side streets for authentic city life
